# Word Into Money — Nano Banana 2 Image Prompts

Put generated images in this folder: `/public/imgs/word-into-money/`

Name them: `feed-01.jpg` through `feed-10.jpg`

All prompts are 16:9 or 4:3 landscape. No headline text overlays. No CTA buttons. These are pure visual feed imagery — captivating, editorial, scroll-stopping.

---

## 1. Surreal: Golden Typewriter in Dark Void
**Filename:** `feed-01.jpg`
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9

```
Create a 16:9 landscape image. A striking visual: a vintage golden typewriter sitting on a dark reflective surface in the middle of a vast, infinite dark space. The typewriter glows with warm amber-gold light from within, casting golden light rays and reflections on the dark mirror-like floor beneath it. A single sheet of paper emerges from the typewriter with faint golden text visible. The background is deep black fading to dark charcoal, with subtle golden particles floating in the air like dust caught in light. The typewriter is the only object in the frame. Cinematic wide shot, vivid saturated gold tones against deep black, dramatic volumetric light beams emanating from the typewriter. Surreal commercial photography, hyper-clean composition, photorealistic, 2K resolution.
```

**Purpose:** Hero-area visual or after the opening hook. The typewriter = words. The gold = money. Words Into Money as a single image.

**Purpose:** Near the opening or after the hero. The act of writing = creating light/gold. The most intimate, powerful visual in the set.

## Usage in the Feed

Insert these as `ImageCard` components throughout `feedContent.ts`:

```typescript
{
    id: 'visual-01',
    type: 'image',
    animation: 'scale',
    data: {
        src: '/imgs/word-into-money/feed-01.jpg',
        alt: 'Golden typewriter glowing in darkness',
        caption: 'Your words have value. This workshop shows you how to unlock it.',
    },
},
```
